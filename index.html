LIVE TRACKING


Now there is no need to wonder where a particular train might be. Through GPS technology, get real-time location updates of any train, you want, that too on your fingertips!

This feature makes the train journey a lot more safer for everyone.

SPEED


How to find out if a particular train is moving faster or slower?

Well the speed determination feature solves this problem. Get instantneous speed values throughout the course of the travel of the locomotive.

ESTIMATED TIME OF ARRIVAL


Since we acquired the position and speed of the subject train at any given point in time then gauging the Estimated Time Of Arrival is just a few lines of code away!

Thus ETA viewing has also been incorporated for the users.
